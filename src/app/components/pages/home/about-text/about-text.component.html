About Mohan Lanka Foundation.

Mohan Lanka Foundation, an Sri Lankan social development non-profit organization, is directly benefitting over millions of children and their families every year. We have more than 100 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood, and women’s empowerment in over 200 remote villages across 25 districts of Sri Lanka.
